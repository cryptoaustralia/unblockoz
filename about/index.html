About

UnblockOz was born to provide the general public with practical instructions to evade Internet censorship in Australia

Who we are

This website was brought to you by a forming not-for-profit organisation called 'CryptoAUSTRALIA'

We are a group of activists currently forming a not-for-profit organisation to advocate for digital rights and freedom of speech on the Internet. We all have experience with running talks and workshops such as CryptoParties in Sydney and Melbourne.

Visit our upcoming website to read more about us and our projects: cryptoaustralia.org.au

You can email us on  hello@unblockoz.org
